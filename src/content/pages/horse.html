Physiotherapy for Horses

Supporting horse health, performance & well-being

Is your horse feeling stiff, uncomfortable, or not performing as well as usual? Whether they’re recovering from an injury, dealing with muscle tension, or showing subtle signs of discomfort, physiotherapy can make a difference!

By combining soft tissue therapy, joint mobilization, high-power laser therapy, and targeted exercises, I help reduce pain, improve movement, and support recovery. A full assessment—both in motion and through hands-on examination—allows me to identify problem areas and create a treatment plan that helps your horse feel and perform at their best.
