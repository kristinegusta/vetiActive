Physiotherapy for Hounds

Dog rehabilitation & physiotherapy

Is your four-legged friend not as eager for walks lately? Whether they're feeling stiff from arthritis, recovering from surgery, or just not moving as freely as before, physiotherapy can help!

Through hands-on treatment and tailored exercises, I work to reduce your dog's pain, improve mobility, and restore comfort. My goal is to help them move more easily so you can both get back to enjoying walks, playtime, and everyday adventures together.

  • Movement therapy

    I focus on improving your dog's mobility, restoring their ability to walk, run, and play with ease.

  • Injury rehabilitation

    I help your dog recover from injuries, whether they're recent or from past trauma, with personalized care plans.

  • Post-surgery recovery

    Assist your dog in recovering after surgery with specialized exercises and treatment to reduce pain and promote healing.

  • Arthritis management

    I offer effective strategies to help manage arthritis, easing discomfort and improving your dog's quality of life.
