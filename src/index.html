About 3H-Physio

At 3HPhysio, we specialize in physiotherapy for horses, hounds, and humans—helping them move better, feel better, and live happier lives.

Founded by Elizabete Brauna, a dedicated physiotherapist with expertise in both human and animal rehabilitation, 3HPhysio offers tailored treatments that focus on mobility, strength, and overall well-being. With a lifelong passion for animals and years of experience in equestrian sports and physiotherapy, Elizabete combines knowledge and care to support every patient’s unique needs.

Whether you're looking to improve your horse’s performance, help your dog recover from an injury, or enhance your own movement, 3HPhysio is here to help.
